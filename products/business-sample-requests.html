Product Specifications

Color: White. 
Pattern: Solid, no pattern.
Thread Count: N/A, non-woven fabric.
Care and Cleaning: Disposable, do not wash.  

Benefits: Hypoallergenic, biodegradable, chemical-free, sustainable.
Durability: Use up to two weeks, results may vary.
Contents: Sheet sets contain 1 fitted sheet, 1 flat sheet, 1 pillowcase. For Queen and King sets, 2 pillowcases included.

Packaging: Individually wrapped sets in recyclable plastic, not for resale.
Shelf-life: Store in cool, dry place indefinitely.
Origin: Made in Europe (Ukraine) or Asia (China).
Textile Material: 30% Tencel™, 70% Lenzing® Viscose.
Certifications: USDA 100% Biobased, compostable.
